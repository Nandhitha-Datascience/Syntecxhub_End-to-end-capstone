# 📊 Retail Sales Data Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover trends, patterns, and key factors influencing business performance. The focus is on identifying growth trends, category performance, and actionable insights.

## Objective
To explore retail sales data and generate insights on sales trends, category performance, regional differences, and relationships between key variables.

## Visual Analysis

### Sales Over Time
![Sales Trend](sales_trend.png)
Sales show an overall upward trend with fluctuations, indicating growth and seasonal demand patterns.

### Category-wise Sales
![Category Sales](category_sales.png)
Certain categories contribute significantly to total revenue, making them key business drivers.

### Region-wise Comparison
![Region Sales](region_sales.png)
Sales performance varies across regions, highlighting geographical differences in demand.

### Correlation Heatmap
![Correlation Heatmap](heatmap.png)
The heatmap shows relationships between numerical variables. Sales and profit show a positive correlation, while discount negatively impacts profitability.

### Top 10 Products
![Top Products](top10_products.png)
A small group of products contributes heavily to revenue, indicating concentration in product performance.

## Key Insights
- Sales show a steady upward trend with seasonal fluctuations  
- A few categories and products drive the majority of revenue  
- Regional differences highlight varying customer demand  
- Higher discounts tend to reduce profitability  

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing categories and top-selling products  
- Optimize discount strategies to protect profit margins  
- Plan inventory and promotions based on peak sales periods  
- Tailor strategies based on regional performance
